About Us

We are students from Cumberland High School doing a project with URI and made this site to raise awareness of certain types of abuse such as verbal, sexual, physical, domestic, and other forms of abuse. We provide insight to those who need guidance about their own personal situations. We steer them towards charities that can provide extra help if needed. We hope that this site will help those who are in need.
