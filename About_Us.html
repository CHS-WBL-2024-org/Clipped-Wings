About Us

We are students from Cumberland High School doing a project with URI and made this site to raise awareness of certain types of abuse such as verbal, sexual, physical, domestic, and other forms of abuse. We provide insight to those who need guidance about their own personal situations. We steer them towards charities that can provide extra help if needed. Below are links to YouTube videos that will provide more information about the charities. Some are full on descriptions, others being victim's life experiences. We enourage you to watch them and learn more about the charities.
