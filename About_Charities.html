About Charities

Below are links to YouTube videos that will provide more information about the charities. Some are full on descriptions,
others being victim's life experiences. We enourage you to watch them and learn more about the charities.


  • First Woman's Aid Video


    • This video shows off what an actual room in the shelter looks like and a real person who has been staying there since she was 18.
    (Does not talk about Women's aid but is good to show what it will be like if you actually go to their shelters.


  • Second Woman's Aid Video


    • This video goes over what projects the charity did and the statistics of the services
    that they provide and how many people have been affected for the year 2018.


  • Safe Horizon Video


    • Since 1978, Safe Horizons has been able to help people get out of their troubled situations, and help people find out
    what traumas can be found out and taken care of in a safe space until they have managed to recover. Everyone gets the help they need.



  • REACH Video


    • Started in 1981 by 4 ladies who worked together to help those fighting against abuse. Because of their efforts, they
    noticed more awareness of DA and give advice to those people looking for help.
    They also talk about the support the survivors received from the legislatures. They have a shelter that accepts men, therapists
    for the children, a hotline, and safety planning advocates




  • House of Ruth Video

    • Micheal Trotter describes his experience of getting into the house/his feelings of the house.
    He mentions the doubts he had about staying at a shelter when he first arrived but
    was met with acceptance from the team even when he was past the age range that they allowed for.


  • RI Coalition Against Domestic Violence Video

    • This video discusses their new campaign "Soar" to lift up
    their voices while also going over some of the statistics about DV victims.
